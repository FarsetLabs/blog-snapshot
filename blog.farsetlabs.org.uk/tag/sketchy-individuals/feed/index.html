This month’s evening will be running as part of Creativity NI’s Creativity Month.  Some books and materials will be available for anyone new to take advantage of.

Sketchy Individuals runs every 2nd Wednesday of the month at 7pm, and we plan to continue for the foreseeable future.  Under 18s must be accompanied by a parent or responsible adult.

If you’ve been watching BBC’s Big Painting Challenge, you might be aware of the Little Painting Challenge competition running in parallel – it is open to everyone, with several classes for judging. Your challenge is simply to render a drawing or painting on a postcard-sized medium of your choice. We will have watercolour and card postcards available on Wednesday for you to get started.
