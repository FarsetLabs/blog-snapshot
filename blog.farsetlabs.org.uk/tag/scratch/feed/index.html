There are currently 3 events due to take place this month as part of EU Code Week – follow the links to register for each one:

  • Raspberry Jam on Saturday 11th October, as part of our regular workshops
  • Making and Teaching using Scratch on 14th October, a practical guide to this high-level drag-and-drop programming tool.
  • Puzzle Games in Unity on 16th October, a very simple walk-through of creating a 3D puzzle game in Unity.

The week ends with the fantastic Belfast Gamecraft game jam on Saturday 18th October, also taking place in Farset Labs! Get your tickets now!
