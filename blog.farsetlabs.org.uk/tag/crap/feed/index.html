All the time, we’re talking to people about what we do at Farset Labs, and often, when we talk about our amazing community, people have two reactions:

  1. Awesome! These people can help me learn and grow!
  2. Ahh crap, I don’t want to butt in on their clique
And so, to try and fix that secondary fear, we’re having an informal Open Evening for anyone interested in coming down and checking the place out. There will be drinks and noms and projects to tinker with, so if you’ve been ‘undecided’ for a while; now is the opportunity to see for yourself why this is such a great place to work/play!
